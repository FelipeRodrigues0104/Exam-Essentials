Who we are

Founded by Felipe Rodrigues and João Gabriel Machado, ExamEssenciais is a revision website aimed to provide students with minimal study material needed in order to achieve high grades. The idea came about one week before our first Computer Science assessment, when both were in search for study material and quickly discovered its unnecessary and overpriced characteristics. We then spent several hours searching through websites attempting to grasp only the necessary content for the International Baccalaureate's Computer Science syllabus. As the frequency of exams increased, we were quick to discover that this drawback was present in all subjects offered by the IB.
