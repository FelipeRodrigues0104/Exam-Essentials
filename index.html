Who we are

Founded by Felipe Rodrigues and João Gabriel Machado, ExamEssentials is a revision website aimed at providing students with minimal study material needed to achieve high grades. The idea came about one week before our first Computer Science assessment, when both were in search of study materials and quickly discovered the exaggerated and overpriced nature of IB subjects' revision material. This led us to spend countless, unnecessary hours searching through websites, attempting to grasp only the "necessary" content for the International Baccalaureate's Computer Science syllabus, but as the frequency of exams increased, we were quick to discover that this downside is present in all subjects - and not only computer science.
